Gender Distribution (Bar Chart)

This bar chart visualizes the distribution of individuals by gender. It shows the number of males and females in the dataset.

Job Title Distribution (Bar Chart)

This bar chart displays the distribution of job titles among individuals in the dataset. It helps to identify the most common job titles.

Age Distribution (Histogram)

This histogram shows the distribution of ages among individuals. It divides the dataset into bins, each representing a range of ages, and displays the count of individuals in each bin.

Birth Month Distribution (Pie Chart)

This pie chart shows the distribution of individuals based on their birth month. Each slice represents a different month, allowing you to see which months have the most or fewest births.

Top 10 Job Titles by Frequency (Horizontal Bar Chart)

This horizontal bar chart visualizes the top 10 most common job titles in the dataset. The length of each bar represents the number of individuals holding each job title.

Birth Year Trend (Line Chart)

This line chart shows the trend of the number of individuals born each year. It helps to identify patterns in birth rates over time.
